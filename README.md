## About me

Process engineer at **[Svante Inc.](https://www.svanteinc.com/)** working on **carbon capture** — blending chemical engineering with data science to improve processes through simulation, analytics, and software.

- **Now:** Building **[PyIAST-Studio](https://github.com/anishk18/PyIAST-Studio)** (adsorption isotherms & IAST) and prototyping analytics tools for plant/process data
- **Background:** Process design, FEED studies, and data-driven optimization in cleantech
- **Education:** [M.Eng.](https://www.grad.ubc.ca/) & [B.A.Sc.](https://www.ubc.ca/) in Chemical Engineering, **University of British Columbia** (Distinction)
